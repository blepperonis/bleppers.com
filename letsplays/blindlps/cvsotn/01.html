Blind Castlevania: Symphony of the Night Let's Play (Day 1)


July 26, 2011. - Failure Day

 Yep, this is a BLIND LP. I have NO idea what to expect/do. I mean, I know a bit of the story...But...Watch me fail.


I've heard beautiful things about this game, and wanted to do a blind Let's Play! on it. I mean just listening to the *audio* doesn't disappoint one bit!
So, here I go to grab an iso...And burn it on Imgburn. Tested to be working (minor music issues) on Psxfin, or epsxe.
Yeah, I could play it on the emulator, but my laptop's too full with other crap already (Sims 3, why yew take so much spaaaace and reeesourceeees)
After a good hour or two failing the original PS1 swap disk method, I resorted to modding my PS2 (cheaply) and finally got my SotN backup to work!
Oh, joyous moment to see the Konami screen!
So, I reach the title screen. Lovely - it has accompanied music with vocals (as to be expected with the awesomeness & soundtracks to come.)
And then I run into a slight problem...
The game states my memory card as unformatted, and I can't get it formatted in game.
Damn third-party memory cards! Curse yoooooou. Back to the drawing board....
Okay! After MANY attempts, I FINALLY got the backup to work on my PS1. *Disk Swap method*
Now, let's start for real.
Alright! I cleared one block specifically for this game (I should really delete the others - I don't even have FF!) And it seems to be going good so far.
So, let's name my file "Finnian" for now.


At this point I used a different camera - the other was way too low resolution.

The intro is full of major skipping issues. I just hope the gameplay isn't like that...



I am treated to a "Now Loading" screen - and then, NOTHING! All I've succeeded in doing is making a coaster!
I give up. I'm not going to make another coaster. It seems like I must use the laptop.
I'll try again tomorrow and stick to something less headache inducing.
