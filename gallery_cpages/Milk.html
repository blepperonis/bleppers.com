The younger of the Antee twins (by a few minutes).

Age: ~14 years old

Gender: Male

Family: Cream Antee (Sister), Steve Antee (Father), Honey Antee (Grandma, Father's side), Mama Rabbit (Mother)

Likes: dressing up and looking cute, pinks and purples, cute animals, delicious food, cooking, and cute/handsome boys, hugs, stuffed toys, skirts and dresses

Dislikes: mean or scary people, gross things

Appearance: A redhead, usually, though his natural hair colour is blond like his sister and styled in an innumerable amount of ways depending on his outfit and mood. Fair skinned, with light freckles over his face. Large ocean blue eyes. Has a slim figure except for his posterior.

Normally depicted with Holstein bovine features.

Personality: Feminine, gentle and sweet. Immature and possibly a crybaby. Also a little bit vain in the sense that his appearance and style is very important to him, as he subconsciously compares himself to his sister. Not very good at being persuasive.
